Tania has been very critical of herself and her performance in her psychology course. She has also been struggling with moving towards her weekly study goals. According to regulatory mode theory, what outcome might Tania face?
-   higher depressive mood
Which combination of assessment orientations is associated with the greatest level of depressive symptoms?
-   low locomotion + high assessment
Pierro and colleagues were interested in examining how regulatory mode would be associated with nostalgia - the remembrance of prior experiences that carry a predominately positive affective tone. Which regulatory mode was associated with greater nostalgia and why?
-   Assessment Orientation, because nostalgia can be used to restore positive mood.
When dealing with complex tasks and jobs, which type or combination of orientation(s) is best at facilitating performance?
-   high locomotion + high assessment
Locomotion orientation is associated with ________ self-forgiveness, due to greater _______ temporal focus.
-   greater, present and future
When might assessment orientation be beneficial?
- when people are required to think about short-term goals x
- when it is important to be accurate in decision making 
- when tasks are straight forward and simple 
- this is a trick question - assessment orientation tends to always undermine performance and goal pursuit
Yuthika has been wondering if she should allow her students to complete the final exam in small groups. But Yuthika is worried that different combinations of student's regulatory mode might influence the group results. Which type of group(s) might be more likely to finish the exam the FASTEST?
-   all locomotion and and mixed-locomotion/assessor groups
Which type of orientation focuses on moving and getting things done (i.e., "Just Do it")?
- locomotion
